Our Community Table

Our journey began at Grand Circus, where we joined twelve other individuals committed to starting new careers in Detroit's thriving tech industry. For eight weeks we bonded over lunch at the community table, exchanging life stories and, more often than not, Detroit restaurant recommendations. It turns out we were as passionate about food as we were about coding!

We soon found ourselves unable to keep track of all the amazing places we were hearing about. Detroit Community Table was designed to help us and you, our fellow Detroit foodies, compile and conquer our dining bucket lists.
